LEADS Chain Features

Create and send certificates in minutes, verify in seconds! Saving your time, saving your money, increasing social sharing. Secured with Blockchain Technology.

Issue

LEADS Chain allows you to start issuing certificates easily using our platform. It allow authorities to create and issue certificates digitally to the owners.

Permanence

By utilizing a public blockchain like Ethereum, certificates will have permanence. They will be in existence as long as the blockchain exists (we mean, forever!)

Instant Verification

By placing the certificate hashes in a public blockchain, anyone, anytime can validate that the information in the certificate is true.
